PROBLEM IDENTIFIED
==================
Login was not redirecting users to their respective dashboards:
- Instructor login → No redirect to instructor-dashboard
- Admin login → No redirect to admin-dashboard
- Student login → No redirect to student-dashboard

ROOT CAUSE
==========
Incorrect relative URL paths in redirect functions. The URLs were missing
the "./" prefix needed for proper relative path resolution.

INCORRECT: window.location.href = 'instructor-dashboard.html';
CORRECT:   window.location.href = './instructor-dashboard.html';

=============
